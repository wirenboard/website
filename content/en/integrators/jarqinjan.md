Jarqin Jan is a company specializing in electrical installation work, engineering system design, and the implementation of modern solutions for residential and commercial properties.

The company also develops and implements home and commercial automation solutions (smart home) based on Wiren Board equipment, enabling control not only of lighting and curtains, but also of all engineering systems, including ventilation, air handling, heating, air conditioning, and more, as well as the building’s energy consumption.

We provide a full range of services, from the design and installation of electrical networks to the implementation of automation systems and subsequent technical support.

Our team is committed to delivering high-quality and efficient solutions tailored to clients’ needs and designed for the long-term operation of the systems.
