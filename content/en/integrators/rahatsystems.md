IT-Resheniya LLP is an engineering and technology company specializing in the development, implementation, and support of integrated solutions in the fields of automation, intelligent control systems, and the digitalization of engineering infrastructure.

The company has been operating since 2015, delivering projects of varying complexity in industry, commercial real estate, and the residential sector.

We specialize in the development of smart home systems based on Wiren Board controllers for:

* private homes;
* public and office buildings;
* hotels.

The company also develops and implements control systems for:

* boiler houses and heating substations;
* ventilation and climate control systems;
* grain storage elevators;
* heating supply units and engineering systems.

We deliver turnkey projects, from concept development and design to commissioning and ongoing service support.
