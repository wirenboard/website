#description
One split type current transformer for use with WB-MAP series of energy meters.

For loads with a rated current of 15 to 90 A.

#info
::product-section{title="Technical specifications"}
:photo{
  src="zemctk05-14/zemctk05-14_current_en.png"
  width="500px"
  float="right"
}

- Rated primary current 15 A.
- Maximum primary current 90 A.
- Transformation ratio 1:3000 (exact value on the label).
- Accuracy class 0.5S.
- Core material — ferrite.
- Wire outer diameter up to 10 mm.
- Weight (not including wires) ~15 g.
- Wire length 3 m.
